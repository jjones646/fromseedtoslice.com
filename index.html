The Seed to Slice Project

An effort dedicated to creating a garden-to-table pizza

What is this about?

The Seed to Slice Project is dedicated to creating a garden-to-table vegetarian pizza. A pizza party is held at the end of the growing season for everyone involved.

Guidelines

The guidelines are simple, and are as follows:

  • Ingredients must be harvested from a garden or forraged from nature.
  • Dairy must be produced from small farms.
  • Bartering is allowed but discouraged. Using forms of currency is not allowed.

Ingredients

Required Ingredients

  • Wheat (Red winter wheat)
  • Tomatoes (San Marzano & cherry)
  • Peppercorn (for black pepper)
  • Seawater (for salt)
  • Corn (for cornmeal, Zea mays everta)
  • Cayanne Peppers (for dried red pepper flakes)
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Mushrooms
  • Basil
  • Sunflowers (for oil)
  • Sweetener [one of the following]:
    • Honey
    • Beets
    • Maple Tree
  • Mozzarella Cheese
    • Goat/Cow Milk (will need to barter)
    • Stinging Nettle (to make rennet)
    • Lime Tree (for citric acid)

Optional Ingredients

  • Olive Tree (olive oil)
  • Cauliflower (for cauliflower crust)
  • Cashews (for a plant-based cheese)
  • Charcoal (for the wood fire pizza oven)
